# Fluent Notes

A beautiful, modern notepad for Chromium-based browsers, featuring a sleek glassmorphism UI, workspace themes, and persistent local storage.

## ✨ Features
- **Seamless Auto-save:** Notes persist automatically between sessions (no manual saving required).
- **Modern Glassmorphism UI:** Clean, fluid interface with smooth animations, dynamic color palettes, and interactive hover states.
- **Smart Organization:** Create **Spaces (Folders)** to categorize your notes, and view them all via "All notes", "Pinned", or "Recent" smart lists.
- **Drag & Drop:** Easily reorder your notes or move them into spaces.
- **Color Customization:** Assign specific colors to your notes and folders. The UI dynamically tints note cards to match your chosen colors.
- **Workspace Themes:** Switch between completely different color palettes (Ocean, Emerald, Sunset, Sakura, etc.) to match your mood, with full support for Light/Dark mode.
- **Rich Text Editing:** Full Google Fonts integration. Change font families and text sizes. Supports bold, underline, lists, and responsive image pasting.
- **Export Options:** Export individual notes as `.md` (Markdown) or `.txt` files.
- **Backup & Restore:** Export all your notes as a `.json` backup file and restore them anytime to prevent data loss.
- **Privacy First:** Local storage only — no tracking or cloud services.

## 📦 Installation
1. Go to your browser's extensions page (`chrome://extensions` or `edge://extensions`).
2. Turn on **Developer mode**.
3. Click **Load unpacked** and select the extension folder.
   *(Alternatively, you can download the `.crx` file from the Releases page and drag it into your extensions page).*
